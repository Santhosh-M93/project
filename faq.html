General Questions

What is your business model? +
We are an online marketplace connecting construction material suppliers with customers. We ensure quality products, competitive pricing, and reliable delivery across India.
Do you provide quality certificates? +
Yes, all our products come with manufacturer quality certificates and test reports. You'll receive these documents along with your delivery invoice.
Can I request product samples? +
Yes, we provide samples for certain products like tiles, paint colors, and wood finishes. Contact our sales team with your requirements, and we'll arrange samples at a nominal charge.

Orders & Purchasing

How do I place an order? +
Browse products, add items to cart, proceed to checkout, enter delivery address, choose payment method, and confirm your order. You'll receive an order confirmation via email and SMS.
Can I modify or cancel my order? +
Yes, you can modify or cancel orders within 1 hour of placing them. After that, please contact customer support immediately. Orders already dispatched cannot be cancelled but can be returned.
What is the minimum order value? +
There is no minimum order value. However, orders above ₹20,000 qualify for free delivery and additional discounts.
Do you offer bulk order discounts? +
Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders. Contact our bulk sales team at bulk@constructionmaterials.com or call +91 12345 67890 for customized quotes.

Shipping & Delivery

What are the delivery charges? +
Delivery charges vary based on order value and location: Below ₹5,000 = ₹150, ₹5,000-₹10,000 = ₹100, ₹10,000-₹20,000 = ₹50, Above ₹20,000 = FREE delivery!
How long does delivery take? +
Local areas (within 50km): 1-2 days. Regional (50-200km): 2-4 days. Interstate: 5-7 days. Remote areas: 7-10 days. Express delivery is available for urgent orders.
Can I track my order? +
Yes! Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email. Use our Track Order page to see real-time updates.
Do you deliver to construction sites? +
Absolutely! We specialize in site deliveries. Ensure your site has proper vehicle access. Crane/unloading assistance is available at additional charges if needed.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, UPI, Digital Wallets, and Cash on Delivery (COD). COD available for orders up to ₹50,000 with ₹50 handling fee.
Is my payment information secure? +
Yes, absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and secure payment gateways. We never store your complete card details on our servers.
Do you offer credit terms for businesses? +
Yes, we offer credit facilities to registered businesses and contractors. Contact our sales team with your business documents for credit approval.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy? +
We offer a 7-day return policy for damaged, defective, or wrong items. Products must be unused and in original packaging. See our Return Policy page for complete details.
How long does refund take? +
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after quality inspection. The amount will be credited to your original payment method within 7-14 days depending on your bank.
What if I receive damaged products? +
Contact us immediately within 24 hours with photos/videos of the damage. We'll arrange free pickup and send a replacement or process a full refund.
