Return & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. Learn about our hassle-free return process.

Return Policy Overview

At Construction Materials E-commerce, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a straightforward return process to ensure your peace of mind.

🕐 7-Day Return Window

You have 7 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return for eligible products. Returns requested after this period cannot be processed.

Eligible Products for Return

  • Products must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging must be intact and undamaged
  • All accessories, manuals, and warranty cards must be included
  • Product tags and seals should not be removed or broken
  • Manufacturing defects or shipping damage are always covered

Non-Returnable Items

Due to the nature of construction materials, certain items cannot be returned once delivered:

Category Reason
Cement & Concrete Products Perishable materials sensitive to moisture and time
Cut or Custom-Made Items Specifically tailored to customer specifications
Opened Paint & Chemicals Safety and hygiene regulations
Sand & Aggregates (Bulk) Quality cannot be verified after delivery
Clearance/Sale Items Sold "as-is" with no return option

How to Return Your Product

1

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned product, we'll process your refund according to the following timeline:

  • Inspection: 2-3 business days after receiving the product
  • Refund Initiation: Within 24 hours of approval
  • Bank Processing: 5-7 business days for the amount to reflect in your account
  • Original Payment Method: Refund will be credited to the original payment source
  • Shipping Charges: Non-refundable unless product is defective or damaged

💳 Refund Methods

Refunds are processed to your original payment method (credit card, debit card, UPI, or bank transfer). For cash on delivery orders, we'll arrange a bank transfer to your account.

Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for products that arrive damaged or defective. To request an exchange:

  • Report the issue within 48 hours of delivery with photos
  • Our team will verify the claim and arrange replacement
  • Exchanges are subject to product availability
  • If the product is unavailable, a full refund will be issued
  • No additional charges for exchanges due to defects or damage

Special Conditions

🚚 Bulk Orders

For bulk orders (above ₹50,000), special return terms apply. Contact our business team at business@constructionmaterials.com for details.

⚠️ Damaged During Transit

If you receive a damaged product, refuse the delivery and contact us immediately. We'll arrange a replacement at no extra cost.

🔧 Wrong Product Delivered

If you receive the wrong product, we'll arrange free pickup and deliver the correct item with priority shipping.
